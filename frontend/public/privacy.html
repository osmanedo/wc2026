Privacy Policy

WC2026 Fantasy · Last updated: April 2026

WC2026 Fantasy is a private fantasy tipping app built for friends groups to compete during the 2026 FIFA World Cup.

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address for the sole purpose of identifying you within the app and displaying your name on the leaderboard.

How we use your data

Your name and email are used only to:

  • Identify you within the app
  • Display your name on the leaderboard
  • Associate your match predictions with your account

What we don't do

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to any third parties. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL database. We store only your name, email, and match predictions.

Deleting your data

To have your data removed, contact us and we will delete your account and all associated predictions within 7 days.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at osmanedo@gmail.com.
